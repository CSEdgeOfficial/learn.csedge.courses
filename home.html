FAQ

How do we apply for internships?

To apply for upcoming batch of internship you need to fill the interest form in that specific domain, after that you will directly receive the selection certificate from us if selected.

How do we fill the Interest Form?

You will get the Interest Form of all the internships domain in above mentioned internship column. Please click on Apply Now button, your response will help us to understand your current skills and need for training during the internship if any.

I have completed all the necessary steps, what's next?

If you have filled all the required field in Interest Form correctly, you will receive a selection certificate from us before your internship starts. All important information and announcements related to internship are communicated through telegram or gmail.

What will be the duration of internship?

In every domain, duration of internship is 4 weeks.

I have completed my tasks well before deadline. When will I get a certificate?

You can submit your task through task submission form if you have received it or wait for it to be shared. If you have submitted the task before the deadline, you may expect your completion certificate in the first week of next month.

Are there any prerequisites for the internship?

There are no specific prerequisites. However, having basic knowledge in the respective domain will be beneficial.

Can I apply for multiple internship domains?

Yes, you can apply for multiple internship domains, but you must fill out separate Interest Forms for each domain.

Will there be any support during the internship?

Yes, mentors will be available to guide you throughout the internship period.

How will my performance be evaluated?

Your performance will be evaluated based on the tasks you complete during the internship and feedback from your mentors.
